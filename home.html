Welcome to Tonkin Architecture!

At Tonkin Architecture, we work to provide our community and clients with socially responsible and environmentally sensitive architectural design. Every project provides a unique set of challenges that inform solutions uniquely fitted to the client’s program and the ultimate users’ needs. We create affordable, durable buildings and exterior spaces that not only delight, but also invite people to enter and promote social interaction and play. We welcome close collaboration with our clients, users, the community, and contractors -- a formula that leads to successful architectural outcomes.
